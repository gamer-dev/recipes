Oats Recipe:

A Healthy, nutritious and a tasty breakfast dish, prepared with no cooking and a little effort.

Ingredients:

1. Rolled oats
2. Curd
3. Raisins
4. Nuts (Peanuts / Almonds / Cashews)
5. Dates
6. Bananas

Instructions:

1. Get a bowl, and mix the oats and curd together.
2. Top it off with all the other ingredients mentioned.
3. Done. Enjoy!

That's all, folks! :)
